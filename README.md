
## Nara  <br /> <br />
The name Nara comes from narration - the idea that every reader deserves a story they can follow and every page is a small quest for understanding. 

The journey to reading for everyone <br /> <br />


Nara is an accessible reading platform designed to make digital content easier to read, understand, and retain for people with dyslexia, ADHD, visual stress, and other reading challenges.

The platform transforms traditional reading into a personalized experience through features such as syllable breaking, Bionic Reading, assistive focus lines, sensory focus mode, word-by-word reading, chapter summaries, memory support cards, and an AI language assistant that helps explain unfamiliar words and sections. Users can also simplify content at different levels and listen through customizable narrator voices and reading speeds.

Nara combines accessibility, AI, and cognitive support into a single reading workspace, helping readers focus on comprehension rather than decoding text.
